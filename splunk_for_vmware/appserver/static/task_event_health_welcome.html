The VMware Tasks and Events Health view helps administrators verify that their data is looking healthy and usable.

Checklist
  1. Check that the graphs in the "Overview of tasks" section look like they are representing a reasonable amount of data.
  2. Check that the graphs in the "Overview of events" and "Counts of events over time" look like they are representing a reasonable amount of data.
  3. Check the distribution of events in your environment with the pie chart.
  4. Look for suspicious events that seem unusual for your environment.
