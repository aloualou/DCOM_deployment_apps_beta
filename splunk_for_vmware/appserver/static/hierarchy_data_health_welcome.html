The VMware Hierarchy Data Health view helps administrators verify that their data is looking healthy and usable.

Checklist:
  1. Check the counts of sources overtime chart and see if events come in regularly.
  2. Check the overview of virtual centers to confirm all hosts are represented.
  3. Check the number of entities in the overview of entities table and verify that total size of your environment is represented.
  4. Check the names of entities in the overview of entities table and verify that they match the entities in your environment.
